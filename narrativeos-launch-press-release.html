MavDB Consulting Co-Founders AI-Powered Reputation Management Firm NarrativeOS™

Josh Bartch and Strategic Partner Scott Gelbard Launch Venture Combining Capital Markets Expertise with Artificial Intelligence

San Juan, Puerto Rico, June 18, 2026 — MavDB Consulting LLC announced today that its Founder and CEO, Josh Bartch, has co-founded NarrativeOS™, an artificial intelligence-powered reputation management and narrative strategy firm. Bartch co-founded the venture alongside strategic partner Scott Gelbard, bringing together deep capital markets expertise and advanced AI capabilities to address a growing need among public companies, executives, and high-profile organizations.

NarrativeOS™ applies artificial intelligence to the identification, analysis, and strategic management of online narratives — enabling clients to build, protect, and optimize their digital reputation in real time. The firm's proprietary platform combines AI-driven media monitoring, sentiment analysis, and content strategy to deliver a level of narrative management previously available only through large, expensive PR operations.

"Capital markets work taught me that narrative is everything," said Josh Bartch, Founder and CEO of MavDB Consulting and co-founder of NarrativeOS™. "How a company tells its story — to investors, to the media, to the public — determines how it is valued, trusted, and remembered. NarrativeOS™ takes that insight and scales it with AI in a way that makes sophisticated reputation management accessible to companies and executives who have never had the tools to do it properly before."

Scott Gelbard, who joins Bartch as co-founder and strategic partner, brings complementary expertise in technology, digital strategy, and artificial intelligence to the venture. Together, the co-founders have designed NarrativeOS™ to serve public companies, investor relations teams, executives navigating public profiles, and organizations seeking to take control of their online presence with precision and intelligence.

The launch of NarrativeOS™ represents a natural extension of MavDB Consulting's core advisory work. Many of the companies MavDB advises on Nasdaq listings and investor relations strategy face acute challenges in narrative management — from managing the transition between exchanges to responding to market rumors or building credibility with a new U.S. investor base. NarrativeOS™ addresses those challenges at scale.

"The convergence of capital markets expertise and AI technology creates something new," Bartch added. "NarrativeOS™ is not a PR firm with an AI overlay. It is an AI-native platform informed by the real-world knowledge of what markets, investors, and the public actually respond to."

MavDB Consulting will continue to operate its full-service capital markets advisory practice alongside the NarrativeOS™ launch.

About MavDB Consulting LLC

MavDB Consulting LLC is a boutique capital markets advisory firm headquartered in San Juan, Puerto Rico. Founded in 2020 by Josh Bartch, MavDB specializes in cross-border listings, Nasdaq uplisting, investor relations strategy, and capital formation advisory. The firm serves clients across Nasdaq, NYSE, ASX, LSE, Euronext, CSE, and other major global exchanges.

About NarrativeOS™

NarrativeOS™ is an AI-powered reputation management and narrative strategy firm co-founded by Josh Bartch and Scott Gelbard. The firm's platform applies artificial intelligence to media monitoring, sentiment analysis, and content strategy to help public companies, executives, and organizations build and protect their digital reputation at scale.
