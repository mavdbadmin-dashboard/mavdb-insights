MavDB Consulting: The Boutique Advisory Firm Redefining Global Capital Markets

Capital Markets Advisory | Cross-Border Listings | Investor Relations

When David Joshua Bartch founded MavDB Consulting LLC in 2020, he wasn't trying to build another investment bank. He was trying to build something rarer: a firm that could actually do the work that large institutions won't touch and smaller shops can't execute well. The result is a boutique capital markets advisory firm that has established itself as one of the most effective partners for international companies seeking access to U.S. capital markets — and for U.S.-listed companies looking to expand their global footprint.

The Founding Premise

The problem MavDB was founded to solve is straightforward, even if the solution is anything but: international companies that want to list on Nasdaq or NYSE face a complex, expensive, and often opaque process that few advisors are genuinely equipped to navigate. The major banks are focused on larger transactions. The regional boutiques often lack the cross-border regulatory expertise. And the companies themselves — frequently domiciled in Australia, the United Kingdom, Canada, or Continental Europe — are operating in unfamiliar territory when it comes to U.S. investor expectations, SEC requirements, and the specific mechanics of how American capital markets actually function.

Bartch saw this gap firsthand and understood that filling it required a very specific combination of skills: deep capital markets knowledge, genuine cross-border experience, a strong network of institutional and retail investor relationships, and the operational agility to move quickly when markets present windows of opportunity. MavDB was built to deliver all four — at a level of quality and attention that larger firms simply cannot provide to mid-market international issuers.

What MavDB Does

MavDB Consulting offers a comprehensive suite of capital markets advisory services, structured around the full lifecycle of a company's engagement with public markets.

Uplisting Advisory sits at the core of MavDB's practice. The firm has developed a proven methodology for helping companies currently listed on smaller exchanges — whether domestic OTC markets or foreign exchanges — prepare for and execute a successful Nasdaq or NYSE listing. This includes everything from structural analysis and regulatory preparation to investor education and market timing strategy.

Cross-Border Listings represent the firm's most distinctive capability. MavDB advises companies listed on the ASX, LSE, Euronext, CSE, and other major global exchanges on the full process of establishing or expanding their U.S. listing. The work is technically demanding and requires simultaneous fluency in multiple regulatory frameworks — precisely the kind of specialized expertise that sets MavDB apart from generalist advisory firms.

Investor Relations Strategy is integrated into everything MavDB does. The firm works with clients to develop and maintain the kind of consistent, compelling investor narrative that sustains long-term shareholder value. This includes messaging architecture, investor presentation development, targeting and outreach strategy, and ongoing communication program management.

Capital Formation advisory helps companies navigate the full range of financing options available to public companies — from traditional equity raises to structured instruments including convertible notes and warrants. Bartch's personal experience as an investor in public companies gives MavDB an edge here: the firm understands what sophisticated investors actually look for in a deal, not just what the textbook says.

The MavDB Difference

What separates MavDB from the field is not simply what the firm does — it is how the firm does it.

At most advisory firms, mid-market international clients are handled by junior teams working off templated strategies. Relationship management is divided from execution. The partner who wins the business is rarely the person who does the work.

At MavDB, that's not how it works. Bartch and his team are directly involved in every client engagement, bringing the full weight of the firm's expertise to bear on each situation. Every advisory strategy is built from the ground up, reflecting the specific circumstances of the client — their industry, their existing investor base, their capitalization structure, and their goals. This is bespoke advisory in the truest sense.

The firm also brings a perspective that is genuinely difficult to replicate: Bartch has personally invested in multiple publicly traded companies, giving him a real-money understanding of how investors evaluate risk, assess value, and make decisions. When MavDB advises a company on how to present itself to U.S. capital markets, that advice is informed by the perspective of someone who has sat on the other side of the table.

Global Reach, Boutique Values

MavDB operates globally, with active relationships across six of the world's major exchanges. The firm's headquarters in San Juan, Puerto Rico provides a unique strategic advantage — a U.S.-jurisdiction base that also offers direct connectivity to Latin American and Caribbean markets, in addition to MavDB's existing relationships across Australia, the UK, Europe, and Canada.

This global reach is paired with an unapologetically boutique operating philosophy. MavDB does not try to be all things to all clients. The firm works with a selective roster of companies where it can bring genuine, differentiated value — and where the fit between client needs and firm capabilities is strong. This selectivity is not a limitation; it is the source of MavDB's effectiveness.

Looking Forward

As global capital markets continue to evolve — driven by technology, regulatory change, and the increasing appetite of international companies for U.S. market access — MavDB Consulting is well positioned to grow with them. The firm's expansion into adjacent capabilities, including the co-founding of NarrativeOS™, reflects a leadership team that is not content to simply maintain its current practice but is actively shaping what the next generation of capital markets advisory looks like.

For international companies navigating the complexity of cross-border listings, or domestic companies seeking to maximize their market profile, MavDB Consulting offers something increasingly rare in financial services: expert counsel, genuine partnership, and a team that is as invested in your outcome as you are.
