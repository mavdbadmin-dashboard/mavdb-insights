MavDB Consulting Expands Global Capital Markets Advisory Practice with Focus on Cross-Border Nasdaq Listings

Boutique Advisory Firm Deepens Capabilities Across Six Major Global Exchanges to Meet Growing Demand from International Issuers

San Juan, Puerto Rico, June 18, 2026 — MavDB Consulting LLC, a boutique capital markets advisory firm headquartered in San Juan, Puerto Rico, today announced the expansion of its global capital markets advisory practice, with a sharpened focus on cross-border Nasdaq listings for international companies. The firm, founded in 2020 by David Joshua Bartch, has seen significant growth in demand from companies listed on major exchanges including the Australian Securities Exchange (ASX), the London Stock Exchange (LSE), Euronext, and the Canadian Securities Exchange (CSE) seeking to access U.S. capital markets.

The expansion reflects accelerating global interest in Nasdaq listings as a pathway to enhanced liquidity, broader investor access, and increased brand credibility. MavDB Consulting has established itself as a trusted advisor to international issuers navigating the regulatory, structural, and investor relations requirements of a U.S. listing.

"International companies increasingly recognize that a Nasdaq listing is a transformative event — not just a financial transaction, but a fundamental shift in how they access capital and engage with global investors," said Josh Bartch, Founder and CEO of MavDB Consulting. "Our firm was built specifically to guide companies through that transition with the expertise, network, and hands-on partnership that the process demands. We are expanding our capabilities to serve more companies who are ready to make that move."

MavDB Consulting's advisory practice spans the full lifecycle of a company's U.S. capital markets engagement, including uplisting strategy, SEC registration and compliance, investor relations program development, capital formation, and post-listing support. The firm's expanded practice will include enhanced capabilities in investor targeting, pre-listing roadshow support, and ongoing market intelligence for clients navigating U.S. market conditions.

The firm's headquarters in San Juan, Puerto Rico positions MavDB at the intersection of U.S. capital markets infrastructure and growing Latin American business networks — an increasingly strategic advantage as more cross-border transactions involve companies with exposure to both markets.

MavDB Consulting currently advises companies across six major global exchanges and maintains active relationships with institutional investors, placement agents, and capital markets legal and accounting professionals in the United States, Australia, the United Kingdom, Canada, and Continental Europe.

About MavDB Consulting LLC

MavDB Consulting LLC is a boutique capital markets advisory firm headquartered in San Juan, Puerto Rico. Founded in 2020 by Josh Bartch, MavDB specializes in cross-border listings, Nasdaq uplisting, investor relations strategy, and capital formation advisory for companies operating across global markets. The firm serves clients listed or seeking listings on Nasdaq, NYSE, ASX, LSE, Euronext, CSE, and other major exchanges.
